# ShopEasy Return & Refund Policy — Electronics

**Category:** Electronics (Computers, Laptops, TVs, Monitors, Smartphones, Tablets,
Audio Equipment, Cameras, Gaming Consoles, Accessories)

---

## Return Window

| Condition          | Window                        |
|--------------------|-------------------------------|
| Unopened / Sealed  | 30 days from delivery date    |
| Opened             | 30 days from delivery date    |
| Defective / DOA    | 90 days from delivery date    |

> **Note:** The clock starts on the delivery date shown in your order confirmation
> email, not the purchase date.

---

## Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a return, electronics must meet **all** of the following:

1. Returned within the applicable window above.
2. Accompanied by the original proof of purchase (order number is sufficient).
3. Include all original contents: box, manuals, cables, chargers, and accessories.
4. Show no signs of physical damage caused by the customer (drops, liquid damage, etc.).

### Non-Returnable Electronics

The following **cannot** be returned under any circumstance:

- Software licenses and digital downloads (once the key has been revealed or redeemed)
- Items explicitly marked **"Final Sale"** or **"Non-Returnable"** on the product page
- Prepaid gift cards and store credit
- Items that have been lost, stolen, or reported as such to an insurance provider

---

## Refund Amounts

| Scenario                                    | Refund                                      |
|---------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Unopened, sealed in original packaging      | 100% of item price + original shipping      |
| Opened, all contents present, no damage     | 100% of item price (shipping non-refundable)|
| Opened, missing minor accessories           | Item price minus 15% restocking fee         |
| Defective / Dead-on-arrival (with evidence) | 100% of item price + original shipping      |
| Physical damage caused by customer          | Not eligible for refund; repair options only|

---

## How to Initiate a Return

1. **Log in** to your ShopEasy account at shopease.com/myorders.
2. Select the order and click **"Return or Replace"**.
3. Choose a reason and upload photos if the item is defective.
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label (available for orders over $25).
5. Drop off the package at any ShopEasy partner carrier location within **5 days**
   of initiating the return.
6. Once the warehouse receives and inspects the item, the refund is processed
   within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method.

> Tip: Keep the tracking number from your return shipment until the refund appears.

---

## Exchanges

ShopEasy does **not** process direct exchanges for electronics. To swap for a
different model or color:

1. Return the original item following the steps above.
2. Place a new order for the desired item.

If you need the replacement urgently, place the new order first and submit the return
concurrently to minimize time without the product.

---

## Warranty vs. Return

The **30-day return window** is separate from any manufacturer warranty:

- **Return window (0–30 days):** Handled by ShopEasy. Full refund or store credit.
- **Manufacturer warranty (31 days onward):** Contact the manufacturer directly.
  ShopEasy can provide contact details upon request.
- **ShopEasy Protection Plan** (if purchased): Covers accidental damage and
  extends coverage — see your plan agreement for details.
